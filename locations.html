Menus & Locations

All Campus Dining Services (CDS) locations are cashless. If you use a CDS dining plan, Tiger Cash or Gold Cash, you will be able to make purchases using your Mizzou ID card. We also accept Apple Pay, Google Pay, credit and debit cards.

Dietary Needs

Campus Dining Services strives to provide choices at every location that can meet a wide variety of dietary practices, restrictions and food allergies. If you have a specific concern or question about what is available, please contact us.

Order Online

Order meals from Potential Energy Cafe, Sabai and Wheatstone Bistro Express. Pizza & MO, Wings & MO, Baja Grill and Domundo's are online ordering ONLY. Use your phone or computer and schedule a convenient pick-up time of your choosing. Quick, easy and convenient, the GET Mobile app is the perfect way to fit lunch or dinner into your busy schedule.
